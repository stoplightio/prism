====test====
Prism is able to respond with an empty response when authorization fails (even though Accept header was provided).
====spec====
openapi: 3.0.2
paths:
  /users:
    get:
      description: "abc"
      responses:
        401:
          description: "abc"
components:
  securitySchemes:
    Basic:
      type: http
      scheme: basic
security:
  - Basic: []
====server====
mock -p 4010
====command====
curl -sIXGET http://localhost:4010/users -H "Accept: application/xml"
====expect====
HTTP/1.1 401 Unauthorized
