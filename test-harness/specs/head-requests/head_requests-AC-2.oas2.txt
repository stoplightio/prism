====test====
Sending a HEAD request results in a 405 when no HEAD operation is defined.
====spec====
swagger: "2.0"
paths:
  /todos:
    get:
      description: todos-head
      responses:
        default:
          description: abc
====server====
mock -p 4010 ${document}
====command====
curl --head http://localhost:4010/todos
====expect====
HTTP/1.1 405 Method Not Allowed
