====test====
When I send a request to an operation with Accept header containing */*
And this operation produces a response with content-type of */*
Then I should get back a response with Content-Type of */*
====spec====
swagger: "2.0"
paths:
  /todos:
    get:
      responses:
        200:
          description: Get Todo Items
          examples:
            '*/*': hello
====server====
mock -p 4010
====command====
curl -i http://localhost:4010/todos -H "accept: */*"
====expect====
HTTP/1.1 200 OK
content-type: */*
content-length: 5
Connection: keep-alive

hello
